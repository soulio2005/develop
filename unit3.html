3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

The best way to improve productivity and efficiency is to track the time and maybe restrict the time spent on tasks. Try not to multitask, make a habit of committing to a single task and finish it before moving onto your next task. Also do not strive for perfection as the reality is nothing is ever perfect. Try not to waste time chasing this illusion. Put your phone and other distractions are out of eyesight or ear shot because when you are in the zone you don't want to be interrupted, so turn off notifications.Researcher say you should work in 90 minute intervals and they also after 4.5 hours you will be less effective.
